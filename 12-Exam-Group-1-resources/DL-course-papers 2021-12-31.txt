	https://jmlr.org/papers/v15/srivastava14a.html
	* Glorot init: Understanding the difficulty of training deep feedforward neural networks, Glorot & Bengio, 2010
	https://proceedings.mlr.press/v9/glorot10a.html

	https://arxiv.org/abs/1609.04747

	* BatchNorm: Batch Normalization: Accelerating Deep Network Training by Reducing Internal Covariate Shift, Ioffe & Szegedy, 2015
	https://arxiv.org/abs/1502.03167
